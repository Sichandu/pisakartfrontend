Refund FAQs

How will I know when my refund is processed?

You'll receive email and SMS notifications at each stage - when we receive your return, when it's approved, and when refund is initiated.

What if I haven't received my refund after 7 days?

First check with your bank. If still not received, contact us at pisakarthelp@gmail.com with your order number for assistance.

Can I change my refund method after requesting?

Yes, contact our support team immediately if you need to change from wallet credit to original payment method or vice versa.

Are there any restocking fees?

No, PisaKart doesn't charge restocking fees for eligible returns. Only original shipping charges (if any) are not refunded.
