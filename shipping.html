Shipping Policy

Fast, reliable delivery with PisaKart

1. Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-3 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after payment confirmation. You’ll receive a tracking email once your order ships.

2. Shipping Methods & Delivery Times

We offer the following shipping:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-7 business days

Delivery times may vary due to carrier delays or location.

3. Shipping Rates

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout. Free shipping applies on orders over ₹599.

4. Tracking Your Order

Once shipped, you’ll receive a tracking link via email. For issues, contact pisakarthelp@gmail.com.

5. Undeliverable Packages

If a package is returned due to an incorrect address, we’ll contact you for reshipping (additional fees may apply).
