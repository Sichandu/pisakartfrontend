Your Security is Our Priority

Shop with confidence on PisaKart

256-bit SSL Encryption

All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted with industry-standard 256-bit SSL technology, the same level of security used by banks.

Payment Security

We never store your full payment details. All transactions are processed through PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways.

Account Protection

Two-factor authentication available. We monitor for suspicious activity and notify you of unrecognized logins.

Data Privacy

Your personal information is protected under our strict privacy policy. We don't sell your data to third parties.

Safe Shopping Tips

While we handle security on our end, here's how you can protect yourself:

Strong Passwords

Use unique, complex passwords for your PisaKart account and change them regularly.

Secure Networks

Avoid making purchases on public Wi-Fi networks. Use a VPN if necessary.

Phishing Alerts

We'll never ask for your password via email. Report suspicious messages to us immediately.

Device Security

Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.
