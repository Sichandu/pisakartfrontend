Order Cancellation Policy

Need to cancel an order? Here's how it works

1-Hour Cancellation Window

You can cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it. After this window, cancellations may not be possible if processing has begun.

When You Can Cancel

  • Within 1 hour of order placement
  • Before your order enters "shipped" status
  • Before shipment preparation begins

When You Can't Cancel

  • After 1 hour from order placement
  • Once order status changes to "shipped"
  • After shipment has been dispatched

Note: For orders paid by Cash on Delivery (COD), cancellations must be made within 30 minutes of order placement due to faster processing times.

How to Cancel Your Order

Through Your Pisa code

Go to "My Orders" in your PisaKart account, select the order you want to cancel, and click "Cancel Order."

Via Customer Support

Contact our support team at pisakarthelp@gmail.com or WhatsApp +91-8185843878 for immediate assistance with cancellation.

Confirmation

You'll receive WhatsApp or SMS confirmation once your cancellation is processed. Refunds (if applicable) will be initiated immediately.

Important: If your order has already shipped but you wish to return it, please see our Return Policy instead.

Cancellation FAQs

What if I miss the 1-hour cancellation window?

If your order is already being processed, you may not be able to cancel it. In this case, you can refuse delivery when it arrives or initiate a return after receiving the package.

How long does the refund take after cancellation?

Refunds are processed within 1 hour of cancellation. The time to reflect in your account is instant (typically 15-30 minutes).

Can I cancel part of my order?

Currently, we only support full order cancellations. If you need to modify your order, please cancel and place a new order with the correct items.
